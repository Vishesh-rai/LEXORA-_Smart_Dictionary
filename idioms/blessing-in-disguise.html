A Blessing in Disguise

Meaning

Something that seems bad at first but turns out to be good later.

Memory Trick

You lost a job → later got a better one. Hidden blessing.

Examples

  • Missing that flight was a blessing in disguise.
  • Losing the match became a blessing in disguise.
  • His failure turned out to be a blessing in disguise.
  • The delay was a blessing in disguise.
  • The accident led him to a better career — blessing in disguise.
