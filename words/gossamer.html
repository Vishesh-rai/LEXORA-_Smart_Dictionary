Gossamer

/ˈɡɒsəmər/

Simple Meaning

Very light, thin and delicate — bahut hi patla aur halka.

Explanation

Gossamer kisi aisi cheez ko describe karta hai jo extremely thin, soft aur almost transparent ho.

Jaise spider web, fog, feather ya delicate kapda — sab gossamer feel dete hain.

Gossamer = ultra light + delicate + airy

Memory Trick

Gossamer → sounds like “gas-summer” ☁️

Socho hawa me udta patla sa silk thread — bilkul halka!

Use Cases

  • Spider webs
  • Fog & mist
  • Light fabrics
  • Soft textures
  • Delicate beauty descriptions

Examples

  • The morning sun shone through gossamer webs.
  • She wore a gossamer silk dress.
  • A gossamer mist covered the valley.
  • The wings were gossamer and fragile.
  • The curtain was made of gossamer fabric.
  • The air felt gossamer light.
  • Dew rested on the gossamer threads.
  • The clouds formed a gossamer layer.
  • The scarf was soft and gossamer.
  • The scene had a gossamer beauty.

Synonyms

  • Delicate
  • Fine
  • Light
  • Airy
  • Thin

Antonyms

  • Thick
  • Heavy
  • Dense
  • Solid
  • Rough
